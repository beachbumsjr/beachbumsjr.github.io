OUR GOALS FOR THIS YEAR

Number 1. Make a functional arm and hand to use in competition.


Number 2. Figure out what to hand out inside the pit for a hand-out.


Number 3. Finishing the servo.


Number 4. Having everything work.


Number 5. Finish on time.


Number 6. Figure out who will be driving the robot and who will be controlling the arm.


Number 7. Make sure that the robot doesn’t malfunction.


Number 8. Interest people for next year.


Number 9. Always be safe.


Number 10. Be helpful.

SAFETY



1.All members must have goggles when building especially when other team members around them are using machinery.


2. All members must have an adult with them when using machinery.


3. We make sure there are no sharp edges on our robot to harm us.


4. Hair must be tied back when building.


Through these safety rules, we will be able to have fun without harming ourselves.
